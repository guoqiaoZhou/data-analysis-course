# 参考资源：OLS回归

## 经典论文/教材

1. **Angrist, J. D., & Pischke, J. S. (2009).** *Mostly Harmless Econometrics: An Empiricist's Companion*.
   - 应用计量经济学的经典教材，回归方法的核心参考

2. **Wooldridge, J. M. (2020).** *Introductory Econometrics: A Modern Approach* (7th ed.).
   - 计量经济学入门教材，OLS与Logistic回归详解

3. **Hastie, T., Tibshirani, R., & Friedman, J. (2009).** *The Elements of Statistical Learning* (2nd ed.).
   - 统计学习的经典教材，广义线性模型章节
