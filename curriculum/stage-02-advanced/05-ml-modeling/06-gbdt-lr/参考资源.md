# 参考资源：GBDT-LR

## 经典论文/教材

1. **Chen, T., & Guestrin, C. (2016).** "XGBoost: A Scalable Tree Boosting System." *KDD*, 785-794.
   - XGBoost算法的原始论文

2. **Ke, G., et al. (2017).** "LightGBM: A Highly Efficient Gradient Boosting Decision Tree." *NIPS*, 3146-3154.
   - LightGBM的原始论文，XGBoost的后续优化

3. **He, X., et al. (2014).** "Practical Lessons from Predicting Clicks on Ads at Facebook." *ADKDD*.
   - GBDT+LR模型的工业界应用论文
