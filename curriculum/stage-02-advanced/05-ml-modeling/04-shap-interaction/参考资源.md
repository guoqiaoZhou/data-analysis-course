## 经典论文/教材

1. **Lundberg, S. M., & Lee, S. I. (2017).** "A Unified Approach to Interpreting Model Predictions." *NIPS*, 4765-4774.
   - SHAP值的奠基论文

2. **Apley, D. W., & Zhu, J. (2020).** "Visualizing the effects of predictor variables in black box supervised learning models." *Journal of the Royal Statistical Society*, 82(4), 1059-1086.
   - ALE图的原始论文

3. **Molnar, C. (2022).** *Interpretable Machine Learning: A Guide for Making Black Box Models Explainable* (2nd ed.).
   - 可解释机器学习的开源教材
