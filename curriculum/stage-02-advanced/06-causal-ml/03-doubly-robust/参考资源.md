## 经典论文/教材

1. **Robins, J. M., Rotnitzky, A., & Zhao, L. P. (1994).** "Estimation of Regression Coefficients When Some Regressors are not Always Observed." *Journal of the American Statistical Association*, 89(427), 846-866.
   - 双重稳健估计的奠基论文

2. **Bang, H., & Robins, J. M. (2005).** "Doubly robust estimation in missing data and causal inference models." *Biometrics*, 61(4), 962-973.
   - 双重稳健估计的综合阐述

3. **Kennedy, E. H. (2022).** "Semiparametric doubly robust targeted double machine learning: a review." *arXiv:2203.06469*.
   - 双重稳健学习的现代综述
