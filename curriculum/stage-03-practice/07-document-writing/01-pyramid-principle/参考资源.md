## 经典论文/教材

1. **Minto, B. (2009).** *The Pyramid Principle: Logic in Writing and Thinking* (3rd ed.).
   - 金字塔原理的经典教材，结构化写作标准

2. **Tufte, E. R. (2001).** *The Visual Display of Quantitative Information* (2nd ed.).
   - 数据可视化的经典教材，数字表达规范参考

3. **Heath, C., & Heath, D. (2007).** *Made to Stick: Why Some Ideas Survive and Others Die*.
   - 如何让分析结论更有影响力
